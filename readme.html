# **CVGenerateBD** **CVGenerateBD** is an intuitive and easy-to-use CV generator designed specifically for students and fresh graduates in Bangladesh. Create job-ready resumes using customizable templates with guided prompts, helping you stand out in the job market. ## **Features** - **User-Friendly Interface**: Easy to use for beginners and experienced users alike. - **Customizable Templates**: Choose from a variety of modern, professional CV templates. - **Instant Preview**: View real-time updates of your resume as you edit. - **Multiple Download Formats**: Download your resume in PDF or DOCX format. - **Bangladesh-Specific Focus**: Tailored for the unique needs of students and job seekers in Bangladesh.
